About Us

This website was created to support people of all ages during lockdown. As these are unprecidented times, we believe it is important to have access to useful materials that can help. Take a look at our content and enjoy!

Domestic Abuse

The National Domestic Abuse Helpline is a freephone 24 hour helpline which provides advice and support to women and can refer them to emergency accommodation.

Telephone: 0808 200 0247

Supporting the Elderly

Age UK are offering free weekly friendship calls. Contact the freephone on 0800 055 6112 (Mon-Sun), 8am-7pm to be allocated a buddy. Call in times are flexible to suit the different needs of everyone who take part..
